## 1. Spear — toolbar slot 6
A flint-tipped spear: thrown like a rock but it hits **~5× as hard** (was ×3). No stored ammo — it's
crafted on the throw from **2 wood + 1 rock**.

- HTML slot 6 (🗡️, cost hint `2🪵+1🪨`); `Digit6` selects it; `setSlot` highlight loop extended to 6;
  `onLeftClick` routes slot 6 → `throwSpear()`.

  into the existing `thrownRocks` array tagged `{mult:3, spear:true}`. `buildSpearMesh()` is a shaft +
  cone tip laid along local −Z; the flight loop `lookAt`s the velocity so the tip leads the arc.
- `updateThrownRocks` now reads `m = r.mult||1`: lions `−m`, prey `−9·m`, gorilla `−8·m`. Rock throws
  push `mult:1`, so rock behaviour is unchanged. Kill-feed icon switches 🪨/🗡️.
- **Look (batch 3):** `buildSpearMesh` rebuilt so it reads as a weapon, not a rock — a tapered wooden
  **handle** (cylinder), a **leather grip** + butt cap at the rear, a **cord lashing** band, and a

## 2. Rock cap 5 → 10

## 3. Perching costs no stamina
Steven: staying in a tree shouldn't drain stamina.
- `PLAYER.perchDrain 7 → 0`; removed the perch-block drain **and** the `stamina ≤ 0 → dropFromTree()`
  forced-drop (no more "grip gave out").
- Stamina now **regens while perched** (dropped the `!player.inTree` guard on regen) — a tree is a safe
  place to catch your breath and wait out the pride or the gorilla.

## 4 & 5. Grass hides you — from the gorilla *and* the prey
The gorilla and the prey ignored the player's stealth signature; only the lions read it. Now they all do.
- **Gorilla:** `noticesPlayer = distP < GOR.PURSUE_RANGE * (treed ? 1 : stealth.visMul)`. Grass + crouch
  shrink its notice range (visMul ≈ 0.34 in grass+crouch → ~9 m from 26 m), and ducking into grass mid-
  chase drops you. A **treed** player stays the gorilla's exception — it always sees that one.
- **Prey:** the herd only treats the player as a threat when `pd < P.fleeDist * stealth.visMul`, so you
  can creep up through grass to set up the `[Q]` ×3 ambush instead of scattering them on approach.

Both reuse the existing `stealth.visMul` (computed in `updateStealth` before the AI updates each frame).

## 6. Calmer prey roam more

Net: an unthreatened herd drifts across the map. Measured ~11 u over 2 s vs ~2 u before.

## Gorilla: brute + counters (batch 2)

**Trigger:** Steven — make the spear stronger; the gorilla should *smack* for ~half health (super
strong); give it a big detection range ("hear you from far away"); and make rocks stun it.

- **Stronger spear:** projectile `mult 3 → 5` (lion −5, prey −45, gorilla −40 per hit).
- **Smack, not a grind:** the old contact damage was continuous `MAUL_DPS = 18/s`. Replaced with one
  devastating blow — `GOR.SMACK_DMG = 50` (half the 100-HP bar) on a `SMACK_CD = 1.7 s` cooldown, with
  `SMACK_KB = 15` knockback and a `SMACK_STUN = 0.6 s` daze. Implemented in `updateHealth`: while a
  grounded gorilla is within `MAUL_RANGE` it suppresses regen (`nearLion`) and, when `G.smackCd` clears,
  lands the hit + flings the player. `MAUL_DPS` removed from `GOR`.
- **Big range:** `PURSUE_RANGE 26 → 40` — it notices and advances on the player from across most of the
  map. Still scaled by `stealth.visMul`, so grass/crouch shrink it proportionally (the batch-1 fix).
- **Rocks/spears stun it (the counter):** new `G.stunTimer`. A rock sets `GOR.ROCK_STUN = 1.5 s`, a spear
  `GOR.SPEAR_STUN = 2.8 s`. A new guard at the top of the gorilla FSM freezes a dazed gorilla (no smack/
  swipe/grab/chase, lions can pile on) until it recovers. This is deliberate: a brute that hits for half
  your bar from 40 m needs a player counter — bait the smack, then rock/spear it and run or climb.

  *Read of the ask:* "he doesn't get stunned by rocks, can you change that" → make rocks stun him
  (they didn't before). Pairs with the buffs to keep him beatable. Flip to stun-immune if that was wrong.

**Verified** (in-page, zero errors): spear `mult 5` → 40 dmg + 2.8 s stun on the gorilla · smack 100→50
then no further hit on cooldown, knockback 15, player daze 0.6 s · gorilla `pursuing` at 35 m · a stunned
gorilla stays frozen (no state change, player HP unchanged while adjacent).

## Rhino — neutral megafauna (batch 5)

**Trigger:** Steven — add a rhino that walks around and mostly does nothing; lots of health (more than
the gorilla); killable; **if you attack it, it charges** (and if you hit it from far, "it knows exactly
where you are" and charges once); poor senses so you can **hide in grass**; **gets stunned**; **can't go
through walls**.

New self-contained module (mirrors the gorilla's structure): `RHINO` config, `rhinoMeshes[]`,
`makeRhino` / `spawnRhino` / `animateRhino` / `rhinoStep` / `updateRhinos`. Wired into the array list,
the `animate()` update order (after `updateGorillas`), `resetGame` (cleanup + reset + spawn 2), and the
`updateThrownRocks` damage loop.

- **Mesh:** a grey low-poly rhino — long low body + shoulder hump, lowered head + snout, **two horns**
  (big front + small rear cone), ears, tail, four stout hip-pivot legs (flat children, reuses the shared
  hit-flash). Scale 1.1, the largest animal footprint.
- **Roam:** slow amble (`SPEED_ROAM 3`) with ~35 % head-down pauses; turns back at the world edge.
- **Senses:** poor — only an **exposed** player within `SENSE_RANGE 7 × stealth.visMul` sets it off, so
  grass/crouch hide you (in grass+crouch the range is ~2 m). Treed player is ignored.
- **Provoked → charge:** a rock/spear hit deals damage, **stuns** it (`ROCK_STUN 1.2` / `SPEAR_STUN 2.2`),
  and records your **exact** position; once the stun clears it makes one committed straight-line charge
  (`SPEED_CHARGE 16`, very low turn) at that spot, goring a grounded player for `CHARGE_DMG 35` +
  `CHARGE_KB 18` + a `0.7 s` daze. The charge ends on contact, on reaching the spot, on a wall/edge, or
  after `CHARGE_RANGE_MAX 34`; then `CHARGE_CD 2.5 s` keeps it calm so it's "once".
- **Walls:** `rhinoStep` runs `collideWalls(pos, BODY_R 1.1)` every frame and reports *forward progress*;
  a charge that stalls against a wall/edge ends. It **cannot** smash through like the gorilla.
- **Tanky:** `HEALTH 220` (> gorilla 160); spear `SPEAR_DMG 16` → ~14 spears, rock `ROCK_DMG 8` + stun.
  Down one → a big edible carcass (food 90).

**Verified** (in-page, zero errors): 2 spawn at reset · roams ~3 u/s · passive sense charges at visMul 1,
ignores at visMul 0.3 · spear hit → 16 dmg + 2.2 s stun + remembers exact spot, then charges · charge

### Rhino → a prey that fights (batch 6)
Reworked from "neutral, charges once" into a **combatant in the food web**.
- **Lions & the gorilla attack it.** Lions get a `fight_rhino` state (mirrors `fight_gorilla`: approach &
  hold within 2.2 m); the gorilla's engage foe-scan now includes grounded rhinos (`foe`/`fd`), and its
  swipe damages a rhino + flips the rhino's target to the gorilla. Lions adjacent (<2.6 m) deal
  `LION_DPS 8` each to the rhino.
- **It fights back.** New `target`/`targetKind` (player|lion|gorilla): the rhino locks onto its current
  attacker (lion biters / the gorilla's swipe / a thrown weapon) or an exposed nearby player, and **keeps
  charging** it (homing toward the target's *current* position at `SPEED_CHARGE`, `TURN_CHARGE 2.2` so it
  tracks) instead of one charge at a fixed spot. Gores the player for `SMACK_DMG 35`+knockback+daze, or a
  lion/gorilla for `GORE_DMG 22`+stun.
- **Turn-to-face (no more hits from behind).** Inside `MELEE_RANGE` it stops and pivots toward the target
  and only gores when `facing > FACE_DOT` (~60°). Verified: player placed behind → no hit (hp 100), then
  the rhino turns and gores (hp 65).
- **Still:** stunned by rocks/spears, blocked by walls (charge gives up after `BLOCK_GIVEUP 2 s` pinned),
  hidden from by grass/crouch (`SENSE_RANGE × stealth.visMul`).
- **Look pass:** richer two-tone mesh — rounded back over a darker belly, shoulder/hip skin-fold plates,
  brow ridge, neck, bigger angled horns, ears, hoofed tapered legs (`rhino_render.png` /
  `render_rhino_compare.py`).

**Verified** (in-page, zero errors): lions approach & bite (rhino took dmg) and get gored back (lion

### Escape windows, reheal & spawn cadence (batch 7)
- **Rhino slows after a hit** so it's escapable: on goring the player it sets `slowT = SLOW_AFTER_HIT 2.8 s`,
  during which its charge drops to `SPEED_SLOW 6` (from 15). Still chases — you can just outrun it now.
- **No cheap hits.** It won't gore the player through a wall (`segHitsWall` line-of-sight on the smack) or
  while you're treed / grappling / climbing / up on a wall (`playerOffGround` — also drops the player as a
  target so it stops trying). Verified: wall between → no hit; grappling → target invalid, no hit.
- **Animals run when about to die.** Prey below 30 % HP force `flee` (panic-run even with no predator in
  range); the rhino below `FLEE_HP 25 %` breaks off its target and runs from the nearest threat.
- **Daily reheal.** `healAllAnimals()` restores every lion/prey/gorilla/rhino to full at each day↔night
  turn (`updateDayNight`); a "Day N — wildlife recovered" feed at dawn.
- **Rhinos on the minimap** — grey dot, red when locked onto you (charging).
- **Rhino spawn cadence**: a new one wanders in every **2–5 in-game days** (`dn.nextRhinoDay`), `MAX 3`.
