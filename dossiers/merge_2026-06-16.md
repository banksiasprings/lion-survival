# Merge dossier — reuniting the gorilla AI with the animals

**Performed:** 2026-06-17 (work spanned the 2026-06-16 gorilla session)
**Outcome:** one canonical `index.html` = full animal roster **+** advanced apex-gorilla AI.

## Why this was needed

A folder got moved mid-development, so two parallel `index.html` lineages grew from a
common Phase-5 ancestor and never reconciled:

- one line kept the **animals** (Phase 6 prey + food chain) but only a *basic* gorilla;
- the other line grew the **advanced gorilla AI** but on a pre-prey base, so it had *no animals*.

Steven asked to "put the games together" — take the union: all animals **and** the good gorilla.

## Merge strategy

The two gorilla systems were **incompatible forks** (different identifiers: `gorillas`/`GORILLA`
vs `gorillaMeshes`/`GOR`; different state machines). The prey/food-chain system is far larger and
more deeply woven into lion AI/HUD/minimap than the gorilla, so the smaller, safer operation was:

> **Keep `index.html` (animals) as the base. Excise the basic gorilla. Transplant the advanced
> gorilla verbatim from `index copy 2.html`, then rewire the integration seams.**

Common-ancestor reasoning: `index.html = ancestor + prey + basic-gorilla`;
`index copy 2.html = ancestor + advanced-gorilla` → target = `ancestor + prey + advanced-gorilla`.

### What came from where
- **From `index copy 2.html` (transplanted ~190 lines, faithful):** `GOR` config; `makeGorilla`,
  `spawnGorilla`, `nearestClimbableTree`, `playerShelterTree`, `gorillaFace`, `gorillaMoveToward`,
  `animateGorilla`, `gorillaSmashWall`, `gorillaEnsurePerch`, `wallBlockingPath`, `updateGorillas`
  (the full FSM); the Raw-Signals 🦍 row; the rock-chip behaviour.
- **From `index.html` (kept):** the entire animal roster + food chain, lions, walls, grapple/perch,
  day-night, weather, watering hole, stealth, HUD, minimap — untouched.
- **Newly written to bridge the two:**
  - `player.stunTimer` (+ input-lock in keydown/mousedown + freeze in `updatePlayer`) — the tree-grab daze.
  - `dn.firstGorilla` / `dn.nextGorillaDay` — advanced spawn cadence (first at 22 s, then every 2 days).
  - `removeWallAt(idx)` — shared wall-removal that splices `wallMeshes` **and** `wallAABBs` in lockstep;
    both the player's axe (`chopWall`, refactored to use it) and the gorilla's wall-smash go through it.
  - Gorilla maul folded into `updateHealth` (grounded "pursuing" gorilla damages the player per-frame).

### Two of Steven's mechanics preserved (adapted, not faithful-to-donor)
The donor's gorilla was unkillable-by-lions; the base let "a whole pride gang up and bring it down."
To avoid losing that, I kept it, rebalanced onto the new FSM:
- `nearestGorilla()` re-added (skips perched/sleeping = up-a-tree) so lions still enter `fight_gorilla`.
- `GOR.LION_BITE_DPS = 7` — lions pressed on a *grounded* gorilla wear it down; its swipes punish them
  back, so a committed pride can fell it but pays for it. A downed gorilla still leaves a corpse.
