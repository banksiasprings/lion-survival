🦁 LION SURVIVAL

Survive the savanna. Lions hunt hardest at dawn, dusk and through the night — rest in shade by day.
Crouch in tall grass to hide (standing in it, they still see you), stay quiet, mind the wind, and grapple into a tree when the pride closes in.

WASD Move  |  Mouse Look  |  Shift Sprint  |  Space Jump  |  C Crouch (hold, or tap to toggle — hides in grass)
🛒 Your kit is on the LEFT — LMB or Z Use active ability  ·  R Switch  ·  Tab Loadout (pauses)  ·  unlock & equip in the SHOP
G Grapple → tree (RMB/Space drop · safe from lions while perched)  |  F Throw Rock  |  Q Pounce (ambush from grass ×3)  |  E Eat kill / Collect wood & rocks
🏹 Crossbow equipped? Hold RMB or tap G to scope-zoom, LMB to fire. Each bolt costs 1 rhino horn.
 Left stick to move  ·  swipe the right side to look  ·  big button uses your active tool
Tap a numbered tool up top to select it (tap it again to use)  ·  ☰ top-left opens your loadout
Side buttons: 💨 sprint · 🐾 crouch (hide) · 🪝 grapple a tree · ⤴️ jump/drop · 🪨 rock · 🐆 pounce · 🍖 eat · 🔭 scope (Crossbow)
 Watch your 🍖 hunger — eat prey you hunt or scavenge lion kills (they'll fight you for it).
